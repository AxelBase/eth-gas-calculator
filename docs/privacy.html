Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the ETH Gas Cost Calculator, a simple, open-source, client-side web utility that helps users estimate Ethereum transaction fees. This tool runs entirely in your browser with no server interaction. We are fully committed to your privacy and operate under a zero-data-collection policy. Because all calculations and processing happen locally on your device, we do not collect, store, transmit, or access any personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices clearly and transparently.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The ETH Gas Cost Calculator is designed to function without gathering any data whatsoever. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal Information: Names, email addresses, wallet addresses, IP addresses, or any identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Browser details, device information, location, visit duration, or interaction patterns.
  • Input Data: Gas limit values, gas prices, or any numbers you enter are processed only in your browser and never leave your device.
  • Analytics or Tracking Data: No cookies, pixels, fingerprints, or third-party analytics tools are used.

Everything stays on your device. There is no backend server receiving or logging anything.

3. How the Tool Operates

When you use the calculator:

  • All Processing is Local: JavaScript performs fee calculations instantly in the browser using pure mathematical operations.
  • No Network Requests: Beyond loading the initial static files (HTML, CSS, JS), no data is sent anywhere.
  • No Persistent Storage: Inputs are held only in memory during your session and disappear on page refresh or close.

This architecture guarantees complete privacy even when estimating fees for sensitive transactions.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The site uses only Bootstrap via CDN for styling, which loads static assets but does not track users. No other external services are involved.

5. Cookies and Tracking

This site does not use cookies, localStorage for tracking, session storage beyond temporary calculation needs, or any tracking technologies. Your experience is completely stateless.

6. Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, security risks associated with transmission or server storage are eliminated. We recommend using a trusted browser and keeping it updated for general web safety.

7. Children's Privacy

The tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

9. Contact

For questions about this policy, please open an issue on our public GitHub repository.

Your privacy is protected by design—nothing is collected, nothing is shared. Use the ETH Gas Cost Calculator with complete confidence.
